## Objective
Implement premium UI refinements to modernize the Task Buddy interface, aligning with premium visual standards (glassmorphism, improved shadows, white/dark card tokens) and specific layout requirements.

## Key Changes

### Sidebar & Workspace Layout
- **Component**: `src/components/sidebar.tsx`

- **Component**: `src/components/dashboard.tsx`

- **Components**: `src/components/new-task-modal.tsx`, `src/pages/TaskDetailPage.tsx`
- **Labels**: Renamed "Manifest Task" and "Finalize Task" to the more intuitive "Create Task".

- **Visuals**: Replaced grey backgrounds with pure white (light) and zinc-900 (dark) cards. Applied glassmorphic backdrop blurs and subtle shadows (`shadow-sm`) per UI-SPEC.
- **Detail View**: Updated `TaskDetailPage.tsx` to display full timestamps ("MMM d, yyyy 'at' p") and match the new card styling.
